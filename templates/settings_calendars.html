Connect a Calendar

Paste an iCal URL from Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, Outlook, or any calendar app. This gives the assistant read-only access to your schedule so it can plan around your commitments.

How to get your iCal URL

Google Calendar: Settings → pick calendar → "Secret address in iCal format"

Apple Calendar (iCloud): Calendar app → right-click calendar → Share → Public Calendar → copy URL

Outlook: Settings → Calendar → Shared calendars → Publish → ICS link
