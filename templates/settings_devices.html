Connect Your Health Devices

DREAM-Chat can integrate with your wearables and health apps to provide personalized insights based on real-time health data. Device connections are powered by MyDataHelps, a secure health data platform that supports dozens of devices and apps.

Supported Platforms

Apple Health
Steps, heart rate, sleep, workouts, and more from iPhone and Apple Watch.
Google Health Connect
Aggregate health data from Android devices and compatible apps.
Fitbit
Activity tracking, sleep stages, heart rate, and SpO2 data.
Garmin
GPS workouts, body battery, stress tracking, and advanced metrics.
Oura
Sleep quality, readiness scores, temperature trends, and HRV.

How It Works

Once device connections are enabled, the setup will be simple:

  1. Click Connect Device and choose your platform.
  2. Authorize access through MyDataHelps' secure OAuth flow.
  3. Your health data syncs automatically in the background.
  4. DREAM-Chat uses the data to give you personalized health insights.

Your data stays private and is only used to improve your health recommendations. You can disconnect any device at any time.
